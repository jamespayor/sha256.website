sha256.website - About

This website is a tool for computing and verifying hashes, made by James Payor. There are other sites, but I wanted something simple and easy to find.

SHA-256 (and SHA-512) are cryptographic hash functions. They can take in a long stream of data and turn it into a short sequence of bytes, called the "hash".

These hashes are designed to be pseudorandom: without access to the input data, we can't tell them apart from random numbers. So they're useful for proving you know something without revealing it.
